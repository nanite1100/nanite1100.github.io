Laboratory

I like the idea of abandoned, overgrown environments so I made this creepy, abandoned science lab. I modelled the building's assets (walls, roof, and everything within) in Maya, textured in Substance Painter, and set up the materials and the scene in Unity. I used the Unity terrain tools and free assets from the Unity asset store to create the surrounding forest environment.
