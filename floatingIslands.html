Floating islands

I made this scene to practice using Unity Probuilder and Polybrush. I used Polybrush to populate the islands with assets (free from the Unity store) for the flowers, grass, and trees. I used Probuilder to create the islands and the paving stones. I plan on adding other environment details like ponds or waterfalls as well as some structures which the player can explore.
